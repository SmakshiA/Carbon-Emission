Graph Analysis

The graph represents the carbon emission of a dataset from Canada(taken from Canada Government official open data website). There are total 7385 records clustered into 3 main categories:
1. Purple - Low emission(2903 people)
2. Yellow - Average emission(3157 people)
3. Blue - High emission(1325 people)
This means that the majority of the population lies in the average emission group. There are more people producing lower than average emissions than high emittors.
